My name is..

Pamela Szymaniec and I live in Gdańsk - Poland. I decided to change my path of a career and become a front-end developer.

How did I end up here ?

It was almost the end of my studies when I realized that it was not exactly what I want to do in my life. I was certain that in the future I want to create beautiful things and that I'm a little bit gifted with soul of an artist and I know I'll need to be ready for a serious career change. I had zero experience in coding, so I decided to take part in infoShare academy Junior Front-end developer course (read more about this course here).

What have I learned ?

During this course I've learned the basic technologies and tools such as: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (a little bit ES5 and ES6), jQuery, Bootstrap, Sass, ReactJS, Redux. I know also how to work effectively with git. All our projects were stored in Github and we worked with JavaScript IDE - Webstorm.

Where am I heading right now ?

Well, I see myself working as a junior front-end developer in a people-oriented team with a desire to share their knowledge.
